Step 1 · the transmitted file

A signal that says how to read it

The message is a 10-byte header, a 65 536-bit body (256×256), and a 10-byte footer. The bit count is the key: 65 536 = 2¹⁶ has no semiprime factorisation, so — unlike Arecibo's 1679 = 23×73 — it signals “not a direct bitmap.” The framing is self-signalling.

Step 2 · the reversible automaton & the blind clock

Why generation 6625?

The body evolves under a reversible Margolus block automaton (single-rotation rule, active-pixel count conserved, exactly invertible). A receiver has no key — only a clock. A minimum-description-length / compression proxy has a unique global minimum at exactly generation 6625: that is where the scrambled field condenses into its most structured, most compressible state.

Step 4 · from pixel to atom

The size code, forced by valence

The three heavy-atom blob sizes {6, 7, 8} map to the three elements {C, N, O}. Of the six possible assignments, exactly one respects the bond degrees the drawn graph demands: a size-6 node reaches degree 4 (only carbon can), size-8 reaches degree 2 (oxygen), size-7 takes nitrogen at degree 3. The assignment is forced, not conventional.

Total: 42 heavy atoms — an aggregate of C₂₄N₆O₁₂, across five molecules. The 95 three-pixel dashes are the bonds (six of them C=O doubles); the 58 single pixels are 52 hydrogens plus 6 isolated points.

Step 5 · the five monomers

Five amino acids — a kit

The identities are forced by the explicitly-drawn bond graph — carbon-degree fixes the skeletons, α-nitrogen placement excludes the δ/ε isomers. All five are non-proteinogenic, but their abiotic pedigree is not uniform: Abu, Nva, Nle form the +CH₂ methylene ladder — the highest-yield homologous series of Murchison-meteorite / Miller–Urey spark-discharge chemistry — while Dab is non-proteinogenic on biological grounds (a cyanobacterial BMAA isomer) and mAsp is a glutamate-mutase metabolite, not a classic abiotic product. Each card carries its ChEMBL identity and primary literature.

Step 6 · a kit, not a chain

The atom census settles it

Peptide-bond formation is a condensation: each amide removes one water (−1 O, −2 H) and joins two components. The decode shows five disjoint molecules with 12 oxygens and 52 hydrogens intact. Among assembly states of these five monomers, only the free kit matches the decode on all four counts at once.

the message is at zero water — the maximum-composition fixed point
Step 7 · the six points

Assembly instructions, not a chain

The six isolated single pixels sit at the pairwise midpoints of the molecules (~13.5 px from any structure). They trace the undirected path Abu–Nva–mAsp–Dab–Nle — a topology, not a direction: instructions marking where the six carboxyls and six aminos are meant to condense, overlaid on an unreacted set of parts. The water the assembly would release is the operation the message defines without executing it.

Step 8 · the Ozma problem

What handedness cannot be sent

The message is a two-dimensional constitution. It fixes the plane and the reading orientation, but not absolute chirality. There are six stereocenters (one each in Abu/Nva/Nle/Dab, two in mAsp); the same 2D graph is equally consistent with 2⁶ = 64 stereoisomers (32 enantiomeric pairs). Handedness needs a depth bit per center, and the 625-pixel census closes with no channel to carry them.

Step 9 · process, in space and time

It self-organises late

The message is meant to be seen in motion. Matter stays dispersed for most of the run and resolves only late: heavy-atom mass rises 66 → 282 px, with 50 % of aggregation by generation 5886 and 90 % by 6588 — 60 % in the final 625 generations. Stacked by time, the field becomes a space–time cone.

Step 10 · error-hardening

Scramble depth is the lever

The message has no error correction: a single bit-flip at generation 0 propagates through 6625 reversible steps and destroys the reconstruction. Because amplification grows with step count, decoding the same target at a depth of 100 instead of 6625 keeps composition recovery near 100 % where the original collapses. The lever is the scramble depth — a constructive redesign, not any single number.

Step 11 · the central result

What a blind decoder can and cannot recover

The reconstruction of the pixel pattern is exact and machine-verified (a SHA-256 round-trip). Its reading as chemistry is inferred, and its resolution is bounded. A key limit, made precise here: the atom census (5 comp, 42 heavy, 12 O, 52 H) settles kit-vs-chain — but it is a coarse invariant. It does not pin which five monomers: mAsp and glutamate share the formula C₅H₉NO₄, and about 50 000 five-monomer sets sum to the same aggregate.

The specific identities are recovered from a finer channel — the explicitly drawn bond graph — not from the census. This is the message's own lesson: composition is cheap to read, constitution costs more bits, and absolute configuration (handedness) is unencodable in the plane. A blind decoder recovers exactly as much as the medium was designed to carry.
