What "re-derivable" means
A keyless receiver holds only the file and a clock. Each card is one claim from the decode, re-earned from the raw bits: the CA conserves 625 active pixels and round-trips bit-exact; the resolved field's component census is {1,3,6,7,8}; the 10-byte header is four 4×4 Margolus states (the rule, worked out); the footer's run-lengths multiply to 6625 (the clock). The two statistical proofs — the blind-clock minimum vs random seeds, and the closed-loop pixel→SMILES validation — are Monte-Carlo results, cited with their sample counts.
